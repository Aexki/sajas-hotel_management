The SAJE Hotel is a modern, elegant 4-star hotel overlooking the sea, perfect for a romantic, charming vacation, in the enchanting setting of Andaman Sea.The rooms at the SAJE Hotel are new, well-lit and inviting. Our reception staff will be happy to help you during your stay, suggesting itineraries, guided visits and some good restaurants along with our own in-house restaurant.

While you enjoy a cocktail by the infinity swimming pool, you will be stunned by the breathtaking view of the bay of the sea. Here, during your summer stays, our bar serves traditional Indian dishes, snacks and salads. At the end of a stairway across from the hotel, the white pebbles on the beach of await you as well as beach facilities with lounge chairs and umbrellas and areas with free access to the pristine clear sea that becomes deep just a few metres from the shore.

SAJE Hotel is a tech-based Hotel where all of our services are available with just by a click in our offcial webpage once you stay with us. Available services include Voip telephone – ideal for low-cost international calls,high speed Wi-Fi internet connection, complimentary breakfast and 24-hour reception.
